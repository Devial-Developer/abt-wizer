Our Story

“Drop by drop is the water pot filled. Likewise, the wise man, gathering it little by little, fills himself with good."

- Gautama Buddha -

How can 'I' carve my own niche?

A question that often makes us stop and ponder, and rightly so. After all, it takes something out of the ordinary to renounce the race and embark on one's own personal journey. A solo trip to enlightenment, and often as we've found, we all need a hand to go the entire distance. A hand, an ear, a voice, or sometimes even a shoulder.

That’s where we come in

If you find yourself at a crossroads, we'll be your guide and point you in the right direction. When the journey fatigues you, we'll enable you with resources to replenish. When it seems overwhelming, we'll help you join forces and collaborate with a fellow hiker. When the ideas dry out, we'll supply you with a fountain of knowledge to spark up new ones. So don’t stop and ponder too long. Take that leap of faith and embark on your journey. Because only when you do, the wisdom tree will bestow enlightenment upon you.

It’s not about the destination, but the journey they say. And in this one, at every step along the way, we’ll make sure you end up…

Our Vision

A Bit Wizer’s vision is to be India’s best platform for personal and professional development through guidance services, catering to all individuals, all budgets, and all life needs

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide an accessible and diversified personal and professional development services platform, addressing an individual’s life stages challenges by partnering and promoting guides such as coaches, mentors, and counselors

Why Us?

A Bit Wizer seeks to dispel the notion that coaching is a senior leaders’ privilege. Using our platform ensures that you are able to take personal charge of your growth, irrespective of your employment level or life stage

Here, you can find support for each and every coaching need, range of Assessment tools, which can be the first step for you to understand yourself before you embark on the journey.

Ours is an open and neutral platform to help you leverage the widest range of coaching support that the world has to offer. While we verify and curate coach credentials, we maintain impartiality in offering you suggestions that may cater to your development needs. Our rating and review process is unbiased, encouraging direct and candid feedback to help guide and influence other users

How Does it Work?

  • Guides can leverage the platform to offer a wide range of services and increase their reach to a broader client base.
  • You can customize your coaching needs and preferences with robust search criteria
  • A wide variety of available assessments helps pinpoint coaching needs and provides valuable data points before getting started
  • You can connect yourself to coaches who complement your ideology, specific motives, within your suitable budget.
  • The platform also provides opportunities for ongoing learning, with regular blogs, articles, videos, publications, and various other learning activities
  • The platform is interactive, with regular forum discussions and the opportunity to learn from your peers and other guides
